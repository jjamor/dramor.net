About me

My name is Juan José Amor (Juanjo Amor) and I'm living in Madrid. In 1997 I graduated Computer Science in Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, and in 2006 I got a Master in Advanced Computer Technologies. I have more than 15 years of experience in Unix and Linux systems administration (Solaris, Red Hat, Debian...), Open Source consultancy and IT team management.

Currently

Currently, I'm working as Senior System Administrator for a Private Insurance Company. I did the design and setup of systems monitoring on IT services, also designed IT support based on ITIL methodologies. I usually work with FLOSS systems, with Linux/Unix technologies, Network/Security, Virtualization, Storage, Application Servers and Cloud.
